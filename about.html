A Copenhagen-based fintech start-up building SaaS that makes RMS-style income investing operational — turning a proven methodology into clear, auditable workflows investors can follow consistently.

Where most fintech adds complexity, YILD removes it. Clean interfaces, deliberate workflows, and zero noise.

User needs at the core.

Founded in 2025, YILD sits at the intersection of real investor pain and modern infrastructure: high complexity, abundant broker data, manual tracking, and decision fatigue — especially for income portfolios with many moving parts.

We translate Selengut-inspired RMS routines into a guided execution layer — clarifying what to do next, why it matters, and what has changed since the last review — so disciplined execution replaces manual spreadsheets.

Intentionally scoped as execution support: read-only portfolio visibility, cashflow awareness, signal responsiveness, and rule-based prompts. No custody, no discretionary management, and no automated trading.

FIRE meets IFRI.

Financial Independence, Retire Early becomes real when cashflow becomes reliable. The IFRI approach offers a practical path — diversified income combined with disciplined portfolio management — yet the day-to-day execution remains surprisingly manual.

Our ambition is to use technology to ignite FIRE — not as an aspiration, but as an achievable reality for the Income-Focused Retirement Investor.

We make the methodology easy to execute end-to-end: capturing holdings, normalising positions, computing relevant signals, and presenting the next relevant action with clear context — so disciplined, income-focused investing can be applied consistently.
